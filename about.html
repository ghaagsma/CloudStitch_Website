Triggers

Actions are performed when one or more associated triggers are activated. Triggers can be activated by blog updates, RSS feed updates, and countless other sources.

Actions

Set up ways for your workflows to react to a trigger being activated. Have a text message sent to your phone or receive an email. Set up actions to activate other triggers in certain circumstances.

Workflows

Any number of triggers and alerts combine to automate notification processes and streamline your workflow.
